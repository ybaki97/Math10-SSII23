Final Project Questions#

Q: What if we can‚Äôt find a perfect linear fit? A: That‚Äôs totally fine! Maybe just point out it doesn‚Äôt look like a linear fit, and you can‚Äôt say much about the relationship. You might consider fitting to a higher degree polynomial, as well.

StandardScaler#

I‚Äôm a little surprised we haven‚Äôt talked about this topic yet, but today we‚Äôll be rescaling numeric data so that it has a mean of zero and a standard deviation of one. Why would we want to do something like this?

From the chat: we want to make things not dependent on units! Example: Suppose I have a DataFrame with one column in kilograms and one column in grams. We might expect that the grams column has numbers that are much larger than the kilograms column. Even though these are both weights, we might be biased towards grams since they are larger numbers.
